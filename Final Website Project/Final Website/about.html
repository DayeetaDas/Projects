About Acceler8

Acceler8 is a flexible award, exclusive to NTU, giving access to a wide range of extracurricular experiences, skills and activities. You choose what you do and when you do it, working at your own pace. You will be rewarded for the extracurricular experiences and skills, collecting points as you go. We guide you through the whole Award on the 'My Acceler8 Award' dashboard. Take your employability up by working towards the Acceler8 Bronze, Silver or Gold Awards.

Nottingham Trent University provides its students with a wide range of activities to get involved in through the Acceler8 and Student's Union.It only allows a student to excel in their academics but also enables them to have vital information on how the industry in their particular field of study works in its recruitment process.
