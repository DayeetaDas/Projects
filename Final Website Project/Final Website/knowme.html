About Myself

Hello reader, my name is Dayeeta Das.I am 17 years old and originally from India. I am a first year student enrolled for the sandwich course of Computer Science. The university has had a profound impact on my life in these few months pf my stay here.I have not only been able to cope with the uncertain weather of Northern Europe but I have learned to socialize making friends from all corners of the world.The academic resources provided by the university along with the scope to engage in various extra- curricular activities via the NTSU volunteering or jobshop has made me more confident than ever.The Acceler8 sessions have given me a compact knoweldge about the demands of the industry. I hope in the near future I will be able to make a change with all the knowledge that I have accumulated

Nottingham Trent University provides its students with a wide range of activities to get involved in through the Acceler8 and Student's Union.It only allows a student to excel in their academics but also enables them to have vital information on how the industry in their particular field of study works in its recruitment process.
